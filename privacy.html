1. Information We Collect

When you use All Tracker, we collect the following types of information:

  • Authentication Data: Your email address, name, and profile picture (retrieved via Google OAuth).

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is exclusively used to provide and improve the All Tracker experience:

  • To synchronize your study sessions and tasks across multiple devices.
  • To generate your personal productivity analytics and performance heatmaps.
  • To display your rank on the global World Stage Leaderboard.
  • To provide personalized AI mentoring via Maamu AI.

3. Data Storage and Third Parties

We do not sell your personal data. We utilize industry-leading third-party services to securely operate the app:

  • Groq AI: Used to power the Maamu AI mentor. We only send relevant study statistics required to generate insights; we do not send personal identifying information to the AI model.
